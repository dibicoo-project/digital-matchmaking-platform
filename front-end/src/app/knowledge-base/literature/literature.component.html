II. Biomethane. Books and booklets
The potential of biomethane for a faster decarbonisation of transport should not go to waste EBA, 2020 Despite all current efforts from the EU, transport is the only sector which is not decarbonizing and even shows an increase in GHG emissions on a yearly basis since 2014. description View PDF Biomethane FNR, 2013 Brief overview about biomethane production, distribution, use and economics. description View PDF Biogas to biomethane German Biogas Association This brochure outlines important technical and legal aspects of the production and use of biomethane. Biomethane is produced by the enrichment of the methane content of biogas, sewage gas and landfill gas. Around 460 biomethane plants are currently in operation in Europe and the growth rate continues to be strong. description View PDF Focus on biomethane German Biomass Research Center, DBFZ, 2012 The articles in this brochure are exemplary focused on different issues and describe country profiles, innovative approaches and the expectations of the producers and purchasers of trading with biomethane.
